Decision Framework

KPI Check & Circle 2 —
Always Stay +EV

The only KPI that matters: Revenue generated — not comeback rate.

Profitable (+EV)
Scale Circle. Launch Circle 2 with same or better offer. If it works once, it works at scale.
Breakeven (0)
Optimize channels & messaging. Try Circle 2 with improved offers. Find the margin leak.
Loss (−EV)
STOP. Fix channel, messaging, or offer. Never spend money to lose money. The floor is always +EV.
Example: Campaign cost €500K → Revenue €1.2M → Scale it. Floor is always +EV. No exceptions.

VIP Structure, Manager UX & Anti-Churn

Retention — Contact
Structure & Triggers

Every VIP and High player needs a direct Telegram contact. VIP Managers must work weekends — weekends are the highest-revenue days.

Trigger Timing Framework

1 Player on a WINNING streak ACTION: DO NOTHING. Never contact. Never offer a bonus. They're not thinking about leaving.
2 TILT during a session ACTION: WAIT 20 minutes. Don't message instantly. Let the emotion settle first.
3 Bankroll hits ZERO ACTION: Wait 20 min → Message with LIVE offer only. Match deposit 1:1. One time, 24h. LIVE games.
4 Next day — 24hrs after session ACTION: 'Sorry, bonus expired — but here's your cashback. Good luck today.' No cost, huge goodwill.
